I'm a PhD student at the **[Interactive Geometry Lab](https://igl.ethz.ch/)** (Institute for Visual Computing, **ETH Zürich**) advised by **Prof. Dr. Olga Sorkine-Hornung**. My research lives at the intersection of **deep learning, computer vision, and computer graphics** — with a soft spot for anything **geometric**.

Before Zürich, I earned a dual-degree MSc from **Mines Paris – PSL** and the **Master MVA** (Mathematics, Vision, Learning) at **ENS Paris-Saclay**.
